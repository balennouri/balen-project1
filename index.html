Football and training

I have dedicated a large part of my life to football and I train all kinds of sports, but mainly football. I'm always training and always trying to do something to activate my body.

I have been playing football at a professional level for a few years now and have gained a lot of experience there. For me, football is a place where I stay physically and mentally healthy, so it's an important part of my life.

Of course, like everything, it comes with disadvantages and advantages. If I start with one disadvantage, the biggest one is that I have missed a lot of time with my family and friends. That is the one that has been the hardest to accept. Something that has helped me a lot with this, is the mobile phone. It has helped to keep in touch with my closest friends and family.

There are a lot of advantages. I have come closer to myself and learned what I should do to keep my mental and physical health in good shape. Sometimes it has been very hard to keep yourself on that path, because there are, of course, a lot of things that can attract you to just do something else. Maybe that day when you are tired and sore after the game you just want to stay in bed and chill and start eating bad things and do other things that will not help your body to recover for the next training session.
